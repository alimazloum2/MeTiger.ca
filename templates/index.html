Services

What we do

Commercial & Industrial Electrical Estimating

NEC-based takeoffs and labor pricing built straight from your PDF or DWG drawing set. Material, devices, conduit, conductors, and gear — counted by system and priced with NECA labor units. Delivered bid-ready, on a delivery window agreed up front.

Electrical Takeoffs & Bid Review

Standalone quantity takeoffs when you need the counts, and an independent second set of eyes on your drawings and quantities before you submit — focused on the scope gaps that erode margin after award.

Construction Documentation

Tender and construction-phase documentation: RFQs, front-end packages, and addenda before award; CDs, CCNs, and CCOs after, keeping scope, cost, and drawings aligned.

Electrical Design Support

Code-compliant electrical coordination support that bridges design intent and field execution, working alongside the engineer of record. MeTiger provides estimating and design support, not sealed or stamped engineering.

Project Management

Scope, RFI, submittal, and change-order tracking that keeps electrical work flowing with fewer surprises and fewer change orders.

Projects

Selected work

Retail Remodel – Walmart Supercenter, Pembroke Pines FL

Provided electrical estimating and engineering support for the interior remodel of a high-volume Walmart Supercenter (Store #2591-1004) in Pembroke Pines, FL. Scope covered lighting upgrades, refrigeration power, and back-of-house electrical modifications, coordinated around live store operations to minimize disruption to retail trading.

Restaurant Tenant Improvement – CAVA Doral, FL

Delivered electrical estimating and coordination for a new CAVA restaurant build-out in Doral, FL. Work covered kitchen power, hood interlocks, dining-area lighting, and tenant connection coordination with the landlord's base building, supporting an on-time opening for a national hospitality client.

Utility Infrastructure – Lift Station Electrical, Hernando County FL

Engineering and estimating support for municipal lift station electrical packages in Hernando County, FL. Scope spanned pump control panels, SCADA tie-ins, generator and ATS provisions, and utility coordination across multiple stations under a county-wide wastewater upgrade program.
