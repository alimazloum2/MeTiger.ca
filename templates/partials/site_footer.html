Privacy Policy

MeTiger Inc. — Updated March 1, 2026

MeTiger Inc. (“MeTiger,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is a project management and consulting firm serving clients in the United States. Our registered office is located in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information through our website, metiger.ca.

Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily submit through our website contact form.

The information collected is limited to what you enter in the form, which may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Company name (if provided)
  • Message content

We do not use tracking tools, advertising pixels, analytics services, or user accounts.

How Information Is Used

When you submit the contact form:

  • The information is transmitted to our server hosted on Oracle Cloud Infrastructure.
  • The message is sent directly to our business email inbox.
  • We use the information solely to respond to your inquiry and communicate with you.

We do not store the information in a website database. We do not use it for marketing automation. We do not sell, rent, or share it with third parties.

Data Retention

We retain email communications only as long as reasonably necessary for business and record-keeping purposes.

Security

We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect information transmitted through our website. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure.

Cross-Border Processing

MeTiger serves clients in the United States. Because our company is registered in Canada and uses cloud infrastructure that may operate in either country, email communications you send through this site may be processed or stored in Canada or the United States.

Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of personal information you have submitted by contacting us.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request access to or deletion of personal information you have submitted, please send us an email at the email address displayed in the website.
